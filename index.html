Election Process Timeline

1. Voter Registration

Citizens register themselves to vote before elections.

2. Campaigning

Candidates promote their ideas and plans.

3. Voting Day

Eligible citizens cast their votes.

4. Counting Votes

Officials count votes securely and transparently.

5. Result Declaration

Winners are announced publicly.
